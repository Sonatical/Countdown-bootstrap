What is this?

Graduation marks a significant milestone in everyone's life. This website was created as a celebration and countdown to the graduation of the class of 2026. For many of us, it's a step towards new beginnings whether that's going to college, enrolling in trade schools, or starting our careers. Personally, I am considering going into a trade program or attending Southeast Missouri State University (SEMO). No matter where we end up, my classmates and I are eager to accept this next chapter of our lives.

Why I'm excited.

I'm looking forward to graduating because it's more than just an end it's the start of something new. Leaving high school means stepping into a world full of new possibilities, whether it's going to a trade school or attending SEMO University. I'm excited for the chance to explore different paths, grow as a person, and take on new challenges. My goal is to make the most of this time, learning new skills and following my passions wherever they lead me. It's an exciting moment that I'm eager to embrace.

How you can join us!

Even though we may come from different schools, the excitement of graduation is something we all share. Whether you're studying at CTC or preparing for your next steps elsewhere, this project invites everyone to join in the celebration. You can connect with us by sharing your own plans, attending graduation events, or offering advice on navigating life after high school. We may be heading in different directions, but together we can support each other as we move into our next chapter, whether it's college, trade school, or starting our careers.
